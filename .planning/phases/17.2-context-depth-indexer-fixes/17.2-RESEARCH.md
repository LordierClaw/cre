# Research: Phase 17.2 - Context Depth & Indexer Fixes

## Empirical Observations

### 1. "All Functions" Bug
- BFS follows `BELONGS_TO` upwards to `Type` and then `ENTRY_POINT` to all other methods.
- This results in a full class output even at `depth=1` if starting at a method.

### 2. Depth > 2 Failure
- Likely due to `JavaAstIndexer`'s `?` fallback for argument types when it can't resolve an expression (e.g. `request.getIsbn()`).
- Mismatched signatures prevent the BFS from continuing to deeper nodes.

## Proposed Strategy
- Restrict `gatherNodesOrdered` edge types.
- Ensure strict pruning in `transformWithRelevance`.
- Improve type inference in `JavaAstIndexer`.
